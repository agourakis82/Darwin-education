Darwin Education

Authors/Creators

  • Agourakis, Demetrios Chiuratto1
  • 1. PUC-SP, Brazil

Description

Medical exam preparation platform for ENAMED (Exame Nacional de Avaliação da Formação Médica) and other medical licensing exams. Features include exam simulator with TRI (Item Response Theory) scoring, flashcards with SM-2 spaced repetition, AI-powered study paths, and progress analytics.
